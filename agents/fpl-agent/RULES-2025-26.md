## STANDARD FPL SCORING

### Goals
- **Goalkeeper/Defender:** 6 pts
- **Midfielder:** 5 pts
- **Forward:** 4 pts

### Assists
- **Any position:** 3 pts

### Clean Sheets (Full 60+ min)
- **Goalkeeper/Defender:** 4 pts
- **Midfielder:** 1 pt
- **Forward:** 0 pts

### Minutes Played
- **60+ minutes:** 2 pts
- **1-59 minutes:** 1 pt

### Bonus Points System (BPS)
Top 3 BPS scorers in each match get bonus:
- **1st place:** 3 bonus pts
- **2nd place:** 2 bonus pts
- **3rd place:** 1 bonus pt

**BPS Components:**
- Goals, assists, saves (GK)
- Passes completed
- Tackles, interceptions, blocks
- KEY: **NEW defensive actions bonus (10 CBIT = +2 pts)**

### Penalties
- **Penalty Save:** 5 pts (GK)
- **Penalty Miss:** -2 pts

### Saves (Goalkeepers)
- **Every 3 saves:** 1 pt

### Goals Conceded
- **GK/Defender:** -1 pt per 2 goals conceded

### Cards
- **Yellow card:** -1 pt
- **Red card:** -3 pts

### Own Goals
- **Any position:** -2 pts

## TEAM BUILDING RULES

### Budget
- **Starting budget:** ¬£100.0m
- **Squad size:** 15 players
  - 2 Goalkeepers
  - 5 Defenders
  - 5 Midfielders
  - 3 Forwards

### Constraints
- **Max 3 players from same team**
- **Weekly transfers:** 1 free transfer
  - Unused transfers roll over (max 2 FT)
  - Extra transfers = -4 pts each

### Formation Rules
- **Goalkeeper:** 1 (always)
- **Defenders:** 3-5
- **Midfielders:** 2-5
- **Forwards:** 1-3
- **Total outfield:** 10

### Captain
- **Captain:** Points doubled (x2)
- **Triple Captain chip:** Points tripled (x3) for one GW

## DOUBLE/BLANK GAMEWEEKS

### Double Gameweeks (DGW)
- Players play TWICE in one GW
- Score points in both matches
- **Strategy:** Triple up on DGW teams, use Bench Boost/Triple Captain

### Blank Gameweeks (BGW)
- Some teams don't play (cup fixtures)
- Affected players score 0
- **Strategy:** Use Free Hit to navigate
